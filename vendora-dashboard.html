Common Issues
I paid but didn't get my Discord role Make sure you're in the Vendora Discord server. The bot assigns roles automatically — if it doesn't happen within a few minutes, contact us with your Discord ID.
I can't log in Make sure you're authorising with the correct Discord account. Clear your browser cache and try again. If the problem persists, send us a message above.
A bot command returned an error Include the exact command you used and the error message when contacting us. Most errors are resolved within minutes.
I want to cancel my subscription Go to the Subscription tab and click Cancel Plan. Your access remains active until the end of your current billing period.
